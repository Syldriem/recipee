Scallion oil noodles

I tried this recipee recently and I've never had noodles more rich in taste

Ingredients

  • Scallions
  • spring onion
  • neutral oil
  • sugar
  • Light & dark soy
  • oyster sauce
  • egg
  • wheat noodles

    Instructions

  1. Slice spring onions and scallions into thin slices
  2. Fry the sliced onions with oil in a pan for 10 minutes or untill brown and crispy
  3. Drain out the oil and remove the onions
  4. Add the oil back into the pan and mix with light & dark soy, oyster sauce and sugar
  5. Boil noodles and mix into the sauce when ready
  6. Fry an egg in the oil and garnish
